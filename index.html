Welcome To

Siyaphumelela Trading


We are a 100% black-owned company which aims to become a major player in contributing towards the development of the SA economy. Siyaphumelela Trading promotes strict adherence to quality, health and safety standards. We adhere to labor equity and encourage harmonious personnel relationships in order to promote team work among members.

Siyaphumelela Trading was established in 2013 with the aim of providing quality services to our clients. It is a family business with astounding track record and experience in wood work and maintenance.
Siyaphumelela Trading offers provides world class services in maintenance for corporate and domestic building, wood work carpentry, painting, tiling and plumbing to mention a few.
