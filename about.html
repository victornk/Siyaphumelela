Business Background

Siyaphumelela Trading was established in 2013 with the aim of providing quality services to our clients. It is a family business with astounding track record and experience in wood work and maintenance.
Siyaphumelela Trading provides world class services in maintenance for corporate and domestic building, wood work carpentry, painting, tiling and plumbing to mention a few.

Our priority is to provide a safe working environment for our customers.
We also have a track record of delivering dynamic and professional services to our clients, timeously, efficiently and to the required quality standards.

ST is a 100% black-owned company which aims to become a major player in contributing towards the development of the SA economy. ST promotes strict adherence to quality, health and safety standards. We adhere to labor equity and encourage harmonious personnel relationships in order to promote team work among members.

Our Vision

To be globally recognized as the most reliable, exceptional service provider and efficient maintenance and wood work company.
To address and facilitate economy growth and entrepreneurial participation among the previous marginalized communities. Particularly increase the participation of young people within the economy by providing skills development and training.

Our Mission

ST aims to provide genuine, reliable, innovative and exceptional maintenance and wood work services for clients.

Our core values are to be customer centric by delivering what matters to our clients timeously, within the budget and in excellent quality standard. We aim to build mutual, beneficial and rewarding partnership between the directors, customers and members of the company.
